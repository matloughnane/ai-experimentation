## What the Blueway is

The Arranmore Blueway is a network of water-based and coastal activity
trails around Árainn Mhór (Arranmore Island), County Donegal — snorkelling,
kayaking, coastal walks and swimming spots designed to let visitors explore
the island's coastline safely.

## The island

Arranmore is the largest inhabited island off County Donegal, reached by a
short ferry crossing (~15–20 minutes) from Burtonport. It is a Gaeltacht
community with a long maritime tradition.
